Recipe for: Homemade Strawberry Cake

  • 1 pound of fresh strawberries, rinsed and hulled
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (285g) cake flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup (12 tablespoon; 170g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1 and 3/4 cups (350g) granulated
  • 5 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup of sour cream or plain yogurt, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup of reduced strawberry puree (check step 1)
  • 1-2 drops of red or pink food coloring (optional)

    • Strawberry Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 1 cup (about 25g) freeze-dried strawberries
  • 8 ounces (226g) full-flat brick cream cheese, softend to room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (8tbsp;113g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 3 cups (360g) confectioner's sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • salt, to taste
